Need these before scaling

Product 
- How well your product is positioned in the market
= Does your product clearly solve a problem or create a new, better opporutnity for customers


Offer
- How well your proposition is constructed
- Are you confident that you could take your product dooor door and sell it using your existing ad copy and lander

Audience
- Your ability to identify and find your market
- Do you know exactly who your perfect customer is? (a perfect customer lovs your product , will not return it and will likely shop again and refer others)


Funnel
- How well you convert traffic into sales?
- Is your funnel able to consistently convert prospects with a conversion rate you're happy with and average order value that allows you to grow at the pace you want?
